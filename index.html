General info.

The course will be taught in English.
Every Monday there will be a new exercise sheet, which is to be handed by Friday the following week. To be admitted to the exam, 50% of the exercises must be completed.
You can hand in your solutions:

  • in person;
  • in A. Limoge's mailbox (on the third floor of the Mathematikon, near Evelyne Bachmann's office);
  • or, by email, to alimoge@mathi.uni-heidelberg.de or ruscelli.francesco1@gmail.com.

Instructors: Prof. Dr. Agustin Moreno, Dr. Connor Jackman.
Teaching assistants: Arthur Limoge, Francesco Ruscelli.

Solutions can also be handed in groups of two students. You are encouraged to work in groups to solve the exercises!
